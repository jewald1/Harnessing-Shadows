* Worldbuffs
  Why World Buffs? If you combine ALL of them you gain:
  +-----------------+---------------+
  | Casters         | Physical      |
  +-----------------+---------------+
  | +18% Spell Crit | +10% Crit     |
  +-----------------+---------------+
  | +15% Stats      | +15% Stats    |
  +-----------------+---------------+
  | +10% Speed      | +10% Speed    |
  +-----------------+---------------+
  | +15 All Stats   | +15 All Stats |
  +-----------------+---------------+
  | +15% Stamina    | +15% Stamina  |
  +-----------------+---------------+
  |                 | +340 AP       |
  +-----------------+---------------+
